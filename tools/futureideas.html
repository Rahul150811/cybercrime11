In order to do this, we need to build a professional website idea or forum, second we need approval from or partner with the government to get the information of the cyber victims. Third We need enthusiastic people who are willing to help others and share their knowledge and experience such as college students, volunteer, professionals.

Integrate APIs or services that provide real-time threat intelligence feeds. These feeds can include information on the latet cyber threats, malwares signatures and vulnerabilities. Display this information on your websites, categorizing it by threat type and severity. Users can stay informed about current risks and take proactive measures to protect them.
