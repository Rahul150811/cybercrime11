Internet Fraud

Internet Crime Complaint Center

For internet-enabled crime and lottery/sweepstakes fraud by internet, you can contact the **Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3)** online at www.ic3.gov It is the Nation's central hub for reporting cyber crime and it is run by FBI.

Consumer Fraud/Identity Theft

Federal Trade Commission

If you would like to report consumer fraud and identity theft, you can contact the **Federal Trade Commission** at 1-877-FTC-HELP, 1-877-ID-THEFT, or online at https://ReportFraud.ftc.gov/#/?orgcode=TFMICF

Digital/Physical Crime

Federal Bureau of Investigation

Submit tips related to any crime on FBI website at https://tips.fbi.gov/ or find specific contact information for the nearest FBI field office.

Tax-related fraud

Internal Revenue Service

To report tax-related fraud and violations anonymously, visit the IRS website at https://www.irs.gov/ and search for "Report Fraud" or "Whistleblower" for more information.

Drug-related crimes

Drug Enforcement Administration

For reporting drug-related crimes, you can visit the DEA's website at https://www.dea.gov/ and search for "Submit a Tip" or "Report Illicit Pharmaceutical Activities

Illegal Firearms, Explosives, Tobacoo

Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives

To report illegal firearms, explosives, arson, or tobacco-related crimes, visit the ATF website at https://www.atf.gov/ and look for "Report a Crime" or "Submit a Tip."

Securities Fraud

Securities and Exchange Commission

If you have information about securities fraud or other violations, you can submit a tip through the SEC's website at https://www.sec.gov/ and find the "Report Misconduct" section.

Customs-related violations

U.S. Customs and Border Protection

To report smuggling, human trafficking, or other customs-related violations, you can visit their website at https://www.cbp.gov/ and search for "Report Suspicious Activity."

Waste and Abuse

Office of Inspector General

Various federal agencies have OIG offices that investigate fraud, waste, and abuse. For instance, you can find the OIG for the Department of Health and Human Services at https://oig.hhs.gov/ or the Department of Defense at https://www.dodig.mil/HOTLINE/.

Environmental Crimes

U.S. Environmental Protection Agency

For reporting environmental crimes or violations, you can visit the EPA's website at https://www.epa.gov/enforcement/report-environmental-violations and use their online form to "Report Environmental Violations" or provide a tip regarding illegal or harmful activities related to the environment.

Food Saftey Issues

U.S. Food and Drug Administration

For reporting issues related to the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA), you can visit their website at https://www.fda.gov/safety/report-problem-fda/reporting-unexpected-problems-fda. On this page, you can find information on "Reporting Unexpected Problems" to the FDA
