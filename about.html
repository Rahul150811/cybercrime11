Hello! My name is Rahul
and I am the founder and creator of this platform.

Reason behind this Project

Together, We can Build Our Nation

After experiencing cybercrime firsthand, I redirected my career from astrophysics to cybersecurity. Recognizing the lack of awareness around reporting options, I created this user-friendly website. This platform aims to assist all individuals, including non-tech users and the elderly, by simplifying the reporting process. Discover our key features, including:

Intuitive Reporting

Step-by-Step Guidance

Support for Non-Tech Users

Educational Resources

Awareness Campaigns
