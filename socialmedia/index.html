Social Media Security and Reporting

Facebook

  • Visit if your account has been hacked: https://www.facebook.com/hacked
  • For more information: https://www.facebook.com/help/1216349518398524

Instagram

  • Forward suspicious or fake instagram emails to: phish@fb.com
  • Visit if you think your account has been hacked: https://www.instagram.com/hacked/
  • Change your password: https://instagram.com/accounts/password/change/
  • Reset your password: https://instagram.com/accounts/password/reset/
  • Remove access of unknown app https://instagram.com/accounts/manage_access
  • Enable 2-factor authentication: https://help.instagram.com/566810106808145
  • For more information: https://help.instagram.com/368191326593075/?helpref=hc_fnav

Whatsapp

  • Contact Whatsapp: https://www.whatsapp.com/contact/?subject=messenger
  • For more information: https://faq.whatsapp.com/479314433984258/?helpref=hc_fnav

Snapchat

  • Contact Snapchat: https://help.snapchat.com/hc/en-us/requests/new
  • For more information: https://help.snapchat.com/hc/en-us/articles/7012305621908

Tiktok

  • Website: https://support.tiktok.com/en/log-in-troubleshoot/log-in/my-account-has-been-hacked

Twitter

  • Contact Twitter: https://help.twitter.com/en/forms/account-access
  • For more information: https://help.twitter.com/en/safety-and-security/twitter-account-compromised
  • Report: https://help.twitter.com/en/forms/account-access/regain-access/hacked-or-compromised

Twitch

  • Contact Twitch: https://help.twitch.tv/s/contactsupport?language=en_US
  • For more information: https://help.twitch.tv/s/article/account-hacked?language=en_US

Linkedin

  • Contact Linkedin: https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/solve/contact
