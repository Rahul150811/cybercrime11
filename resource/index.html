Any Crime on Internet

Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3)

For internet-enabled crime and lottery/sweepstakes fraud by internet, you can contact the Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3) online at ic3.gov. It is the Nation's central hub for reporting cyber crime and it is run by FBI.

Digital/Physical Crime

Federal Bureau of Investigation

Submit tips related to any crime on FBI website at tips.fbi.gov or find specific contact information for the nearest FBI field office here.

Tax-related fraud

Internal Revenue Service

To report tax-related fraud and violations anonymously, visit the IRS website or by clicking here ->irs.gov
If you are from New York City, you may report it here at nyc.gov

Drug-related crimes

Drug Enforcement Administration

For reporting drug-related crimes, you can visit the DEA's website at dea.gov/submit-tip

Illegal Firearms, Explosives, Tobacoo

Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives

To report illegal firearms, explosives, arson, or tobacco-related crimes, visit the ATF website at atf.gov or click here to report easily -> mobile.reportit.com

Complaint against Government Agencies

USA.gov

File complaint against government, banks, consumer services, travel and many more here at usa.gov/complaints

Consumer Fraud/Identity Theft

Federal Trade Commission

If you would like to report consumer fraud, you can contact the Federal Trade Commission at 1-877-382-4357 or click here. For Identity Theft contact at 1-877-438-4338, or online by clicking here.

Securities Fraud

Securities and Exchange Commission

If you have information about securities fraud or other violations, you can submit a tip through the SEC's website by clicking here and find the "Report Misconduct" section.

Customs-related violations

U.S. Customs and Border Protection

To report smuggling, human trafficking, or other customs-related violations, you can visit their website at cbp.gov and search for "Report Suspicious Activity."

Waste and Abuse

Office of Inspector General

Various federal agencies have OIG offices that investigate fraud, waste, and abuse. For instance, you can find the OIG for the Department of Health and Human Services here or the Department of Defense here.

Environmental Crimes

U.S. Environmental Protection Agency

For reporting environmental crimes or violations, you can visit the EPA's website by clicking here and use their online form to "Report Environmental Violations" or provide a tip regarding illegal or harmful activities related to the environment.

Food Saftey Issues

U.S. Food and Drug Administration

For reporting issues related to the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA), you can visit their website by clicking here. On this page, you can find information on "Reporting Unexpected Problems" to the FDA

Legal Assistance & Affordable Lawyers

Discover a range of free and low-cost legal assistance options. Find programs, organizations, and online services offering legal advice, forms, and representation for individuals with low to moderate incomes. A ccess resources for specific groups, including veterans, seniors, and those with disabilities by clicking here.

Legal Aid Organizations

Find legal aid organizations in your area offering free or low-cost legal services to individuals with low incomes or find out at isc.gov/find-legal-aid

Online Legal Resources

FindLaw offers reliable online legal resources, articles, and legal guides for various legal topics. Find out at findlaw.com

Legal Rights and Responsibilities

Understand your legal rights and responsibilities in various areas, such as housing, employment, and consumer rights. Find out at usa.gov/legal-issues

Police, Fire, and Medical Emergencies

Dial 911 or click here
This number is for immediate assistance in life-threatening situations, such as crimes, accidents, fires, and medical emergencies.

National Human Trafficking Hotline

Dial 1-888-373-7888 or click here
A 24/7 hotline to report suspected cases of human trafficking or to seek help and resources for victims.
You may also visit humantraffickinghotline.org for more information.

National Poison Control Center

Dial 1-800-222-1222 or click here
Contact this number in case of accidental poisoning or for information regarding potential poisoning.
You may also visit poison.org for more information.

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

Dial 988 or click here
A 24/7 helpline for individuals in crisis or those who need emotional support. It provides access to trained counselors who can offer help and resources.
You may also visit 988lifeline.org for more information.

National Hunger Hotline

Dial 1-866-3-HUNGRY or click here to speak with a representative who will find food resources such as meal sites, food banks, and other social services available near your location.

National Domestic Violence Hotline

Dial 1-800-799-7233 or click here
This hotline provides support, resources, and safety planning for individuals experiencing domestic violence or abuse.
You may also visit thehotline.org for more information.

Disaster Distress Helpline

Dial 1-800-985-5990 or click here
This helpline offers crisis counseling and support for those experiencing emotional distress related to natural or human-made disasters.
You may also visit samhsa.gov/find-help/disaster-distress-helpline for more information.

National Child Abuse Hotline

Dial 1-800-4-A-CHILD, (1-800-422-4453) or click here
This helpline offers crisis counseling and support for those experiencing emotional distress related to natural or human-made disasters.
You may also visit childhelp.org for more information.

National Runaway Safeline

Dial 1-800-RUNAWAY, (1-800-786-2929) or click here
A helpline providing support, resources, and options for runaway and homeless youth and their families.
You may also visit 1800runaway.org for more information.

Healthcare: Find a Health Center

Dial 1-877-464-4772 or click here.
Check out website by clicking here Connect with federally funded health centers offering medical, dental, and mental health services at a sliding fee scale based on your ability to pay.

Women, Infants, and Children (WIC) Program

Dial 1-800-942-3678 or click here.
Check out website by clicking here Receive nutrition education, healthcare referrals, and nutritious food for pregnant women, new mothers, and young children from low-income families.

American Cancer Society (ACS) - Free Patient Services

Dial 1-800-227-2345 or click here.
Check out website by clicking here Get assistance, support, and information about cancer-related services, treatments, and resources at no cost through ACS.

National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) HelpLine

Dial 1-800-950-NAMI, (1-800-950-6264) or click here.
Check out website by clicking here Supportive helpline connecting individuals and families affected by mental illness to resources, education, and understanding.

Planned Parenthood

Dial 1-800-230-PLAN or click here.
Check out website by clicking here Comprehensive reproductive healthcare provider offering services such as birth control, STD testing, cancer screenings, and sexual health education.

SAMHSA: Mental Health & Substance Abuse Help

Dial 1-800-662-HELP, (1-800-662-4357) or click here.
Check out website by clicking here 24/7 helpline offering information, treatment referrals, and support for individuals facing mental health or substance abuse challenges.

Stay Secure Online

Need a web help to protect you? Check out below

No More Ransom

A service that analyzes files and URLs for potential malware using multiple antivirus engines and provides detailed reports.
Virustotal

VirusTotal is a user-friendly online tool that scans files and URLs for potential threats. It analyzes them using multiple antivirus engines, giving you a comprehensive view of their safety status.
Have I been Pwned?

Check if your email address or passwords have been compromised in data breaches to enhance your online security.
StopNCII.org

Visit stopncii.org to quickly assess if your personal information, including email addresses and passwords, has been compromised in data breaches. Safeguard your online security by staying informed about potential vulnerabilities.
URLScan.io

This service allows you to scan and analyze websites for potential threats, including phishing and malware.
Website Safety Check & Phishing Protection

WOT is a browser extension that helps users identify trustworthy websites and warns them about potentially harmful sites.
Scamwatch (Australia)

This government website provides information about current scams and tips to avoid falling victim to them.
Phishtank

A community-driven website that tracks and verifies phishing scams. Users can report and verify suspicious emails or links.
TinEye

TinEye allows you to upload an image and find instances of its use across the web. It's particularly useful for tracking the usage of images.
Berify

A community-driven website that tracks and verifies phishing scams. Users can report and verify suspicious emails or links.
